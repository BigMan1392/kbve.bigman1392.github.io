## Information
$O is the stock symbol for **Realty Income Corporation**, a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns and operates over 6,500 commercial properties in the United States and Europe. 
The company motto is "The Monthly Dividend Company" because it is known for paying monthly dividends to its shareholders and has increased its dividend for 109 consecutive quarters since its public listing in 1994.
