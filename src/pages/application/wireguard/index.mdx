    - Check your firewall, are you using `ufw` , `iptables` or `nftables`
  - Firewall
    - Wireguard will be operating on the `UDP` port of `51820`.
    - For: `ufw`
      - To enable the port through `ufw` run `sudo allow 51821/udp`
